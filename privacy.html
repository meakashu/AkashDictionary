Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Akash Dictionary collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as when you use the search history feature. The only information stored locally on your device via `localStorage` is your recent search terms.

We do not collect any personal identification information (PII) beyond what is stored in your browser's `localStorage` for the history feature. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

How We Use Information

The search history stored in `localStorage` is used solely to provide the "Recent Searches" feature within the application on your device.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev). Please refer to their privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

We also use Font Awesome for icons. Please refer to their privacy policy regarding their services.

Data Storage and Security

Search history is stored in your web browser's `localStorage`. You can clear this data at any time using the "Clear History" button within the application or by clearing your browser's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
